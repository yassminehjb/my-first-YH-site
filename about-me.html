About me !

Hey! I'm a 16-year-old girl from Morocco, and to be honest, my brain has always been wired a bit differently.

If you asked me 10 years ago what I wanted to be, I wouldn't have hesitated for a second: "I want to be a scientist. I want to leave my mark on this world, and I want to be exceptional." I had this wild, unfiltered childhood dream that I could become someone to this world.

But if you asked me 5 years ago, around the time I was 11 or 12, that spark almost went out. If you asked me back then, I would have quietly told you: "I just want to get good grades, fit in, and focus on the average world."

But if you ask me right now? Between 15 and 16, I had this massive existential wake-up call. I stopped and literally asked myself: "What am I actually doing with my life? Am I really going to live a quiet, average life just because I'm afraid of what people think?"

That question changed everything. I decided to stop daydreaming, stop hiding, and lock in on massive, unapologetic goals. Today, I don't care about fitting in; I care about executing. I am training my brain every single day to make it to the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI), represent my country, and push my limits until I get into MIT. Whether I'm breaking my brain for hours on a brutal math olympiad problem, designing circuits, or grinding on algorithms, I'm loving every second of it. No regrets, no hiding anymore just pure grind and having fun with code!
