SheHacks Morocco - Founder & Lead Organizer

A Non-Profit Initiative Made By Teen Girls For Teen Girls.

I noticed a massive gap in the Moroccan tech ecosystem. High school girls were completely left out of the coding revolution. Instead of waiting for someone else to fix it, I founded SheHacks Morocco and made a my team . We wanted to crush the stereotypes and build the ultimate, zero-compromise launchpad for the next generation of Moroccan female engineers and tech leaders.

Our First Major Event: Sunbeam Marrakech - THIS August 29, 2026

Our very first flagship event is Sunbeam Marrakech: a high-energy, 12-hour solar-powered hackathon where 150+ high school girls will gather to transition from tech consumers to tech creators. No boring lectures, no strict school rules. Just raw code, massive servers, brilliant minds, and the freedom to build anything.

  • The Vibe: Silicon Valley hacker energy meets Marrakesh hospitality. Think neon-lit workspaces, giant cyber-buffets, non-stop Starbucks fuel, and fast-paced tech workshops where we build, break, and fix things together.
  • The Ecosystem: We aren't doing this alone. We are backed financially and logistically by Hack Club Bank (USA).
  • The Mission: 100% free ($0 cost) for every single attendee. We provide the hardware, the food, and the power. Every girl leaves with her own deployed website live on the internet, a network of brilliant mentors, and the confidence to claim her seat at the tech table.
"We aren't just teaching girls how to write code. We are building a community of builders and giving them the ultimate superpower to shape the future of Morocco."
